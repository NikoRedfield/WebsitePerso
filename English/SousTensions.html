Sous Tensions

  • « Sous Tensions » is a 2D serious game related to the burn-out syndrome and based on a survival-horror gameplay !
    The game takes place in a Paris-inspired subway and constantly switches between exploration, survival and adventure.
    A Master MAJE project(Polytech Nice-Sophia).
